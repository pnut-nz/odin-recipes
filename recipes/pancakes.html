Mindy's Easy Pancakes

Description

Here's how to make pancakes.
Mindys's Easy Pancake recipe is super easy and quick. Enjoy with Chelsea Flavoured Syrup - Maple, Honey Maple, or NEW Vanilla, Butterscotch or Caramel.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder and salt. Make a well in the center, and pour in milk, egg and oil. Mix until smooth.

  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat.
    Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
